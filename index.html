NPUA

The National Polytechnic University of Armenia is one of the leading technical universities in Armenia. It is located in Yerevan and plays a major role in engineering and technological education in the country.

Founded in 1933, the university has a long history of training specialists in fields such as mechanical engineering, information technologies, energy systems, and telecommunications. Over the decades, it has contributed significantly to Armenia’s industrial and scientific development.

The Polytechnic offers undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs. It also collaborates with international universities and organizations, helping students gain modern skills and global perspectives.

Some More About Polytechnic

NPUA offers a wide range of programs in areas such as engineering, information technologies, energy systems, mechanics, telecommunications, and applied sciences. It provides education at undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate levels.

The university is known for its strong emphasis on practical and technical training, combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience. Many laboratories, research centers, and partnerships with industry help students gain real-world skills.
